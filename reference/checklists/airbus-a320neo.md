# Airbus A320neo Checklist

## Aircraft Info
- **Type:** Twin Engine Jet (Narrow Body)
- **Model:** Airbus A320neo (LEAP-1A Engines)
- **Cruise Speed:** Mach 0.78 (450 KTAS)
- **Vr (Rotate):** ~140 KIAS (weight dependent)
- **Vref:** ~130-140 KIAS (weight dependent)
- **Ceiling:** FL390

---

## Preliminary Cockpit Setup

- [ ] Batteries - ON (BAT 1 & 2)
- [ ] External power - CONNECT (if available)
- [ ] APU Master - ON
- [ ] APU Start - ON
- [ ] Wait for APU AVAIL
- [ ] APU Bleed - ON
- [ ] External power - DISCONNECT

## Emergency Quick Reference

**Engine Failure on Takeoff (Below V1):**
1. Thrust levers - IDLE
2. Brakes - MAX
3. Reverse - MAX

**Engine Failure on Takeoff (Above V1):**
1. Continue takeoff
2. Rotate at VR
3. Follow SRS
4. Positive climb - Gear UP
5. Follow ECAM

**Go Around:**
1. Thrust levers - TOGA
2. Pitch - Follow SRS
3. Call "Go Around, Flaps"
4. Positive climb - Gear UP
5. NAV - ENGAGE
