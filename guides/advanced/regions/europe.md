### Alpine Flying

**Innsbruck (LOWI) - Europe's Most Challenging:**
- Valley airport surrounded by mountains
- Mandatory mountain flying training (RL)
- Special approach procedures
- LOC/DME approach through valley

**Tips:**
- Fly mornings (thermals afternoon)
- Know escape routes
- Watch for rapid weather changes
- Density altitude considerations

### Scenic Alpine Routes
```
Swiss Alps:
LSZH → Matterhorn → Jungfrau → Eiger → Lake Geneva → LSGG
Distance: ~200 nm
Aircraft: Any GA
Highlights: Iconic peaks, glaciers, ski resorts
Minimum: VFR above terrain, 10,000+ ft
```

```
Austrian Tyrol:
LOWI (Innsbruck) → Brenner Pass → Dolomites → LOWI
Distance: ~150 nm
Highlights: Mountain valleys, passes, villages
Warning: Challenging terrain, weather
```

## European Airspace Specifics

### Altimeter Settings
- Transition altitude varies by country
- QNH in hectopascals (hPa), not inches
- Standard: 1013.25 hPa = 29.92 inHg

### Flight Levels
| Country | Transition Altitude |
|---------|---------------------|
| UK | 3,000 ft |
| Germany | 5,000 ft |
| France | 5,000 ft (varies) |
| Spain | 6,000 ft |
| Switzerland | 7,000 ft |

### Eurocontrol
- Central flow management
- CFMU slots for IFR
- Cross-border flight plans

### SERA (Standardized European Rules of Air)
- Harmonized VFR/IFR rules
- Common flight level scheme
- Standardized procedures

---

## Cross-Border Flying

### Schengen Area
- No border formalities between members
- Flight plan still required
- Customs for UK (non-Schengen)

### Flight Planning
- Use EuroFPL or national systems
- File before departure
- GAR forms for UK

### Radio
- English standard throughout
- Local language sometimes used
- 121.5 MHz emergency everywhere

## Challenging European Airports

| Airport | Challenge | Tips |
|---------|-----------|------|
| LOWI (Innsbruck) | Mountain valley | Special procedures |
| LPMA (Madeira) | Short, ocean cliffs | Dramatic approach |
| LXGB (Gibraltar) | Road crosses runway | Unique! |
| LGSR (Santorini) | Caldera terrain | Wind shear |
| LFLJ (Courchevel) | Mountain slope | Short, steep! |
| EGPB (Sumburgh) | Crosswinds | North Sea weather |

### Courchevel (LFLJ) - The Extreme
- 1,762 ft runway
- 18.5% gradient (uphill landing)
- 6,588 ft elevation
- Helicopter + mountain rating required (RL)
- In MSFS: Ultimate bush challenge!
