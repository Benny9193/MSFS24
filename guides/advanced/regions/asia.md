### Scenic Routes
```
Mt. Fuji Circuit:
RJTT (Haneda) → Mt. Fuji → Five Lakes → RJTT
Distance: ~150 nm
Highlights: Iconic volcano, lakes
Note: Stay clear of airspace restrictions
```

```
Seto Inland Sea:
RJOO (Osaka) → Shikoku bridges →
Hiroshima → RJOA (Hiroshima)
Distance: ~200 nm
Highlights: Islands, bridges, history
```

### Japan Flying Notes
- Dense population = busy airspace
- Mountains throughout
- Excellent infrastructure
- English ATC

### Nepal - Himalayan Flying
| ICAO | Name | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| VNKT | Tribhuvan | Kathmandu |
| VNLK | Lukla | Everest gateway! |
| VNPK | Pokhara | Lakeside |

**Lukla (VNLK) - World's Most Dangerous:**
- 1,729 ft runway
- 12% gradient (uphill landing)
- 9,383 ft elevation
- Cliff drop at end
- Weather window critical
- In MSFS: Ultimate challenge!

### Everest Scenic
```
VNKT (Kathmandu) → VNLK (Lukla) →
Everest Base Camp view → VNKT
Distance: ~150 nm
Highlights: Himalayas, highest peaks
Aircraft: High-performance required
Altitude: Up to 20,000+ ft
```

## Weather Patterns

### Monsoons
| Region | Wet Season | Dry Season |
|--------|------------|------------|
| South Asia | Jun-Sep | Oct-May |
| Southeast Asia | May-Oct | Nov-Apr |
| East Asia | Jun-Aug (rainy season) | Variable |

### Typhoon Season
- Western Pacific: May-November
- Peak: August-September
- Affects: Japan, Taiwan, Philippines, Hong Kong, Vietnam
- Check forecasts carefully!

### Visibility Issues
| Issue | Where | When |
|-------|-------|------|
| Monsoon rain | South/SE Asia | Wet season |
| Haze | Malaysia/Indonesia | Aug-Oct |
| Smog | China, India | Winter |
| Sand storms | Middle East | Spring |
| Typhoons | Pacific coast | Summer |

## Airspace Notes

### China
- Complex restrictions
- VFR limited
- ATC in Mandarin (some)
- Special permits often required

### Japan
- Professional, efficient
- English ATC
- Dense traffic
- Mountain areas

### India
- Improving infrastructure
- Traffic congestion
- VFR challenging in some areas

### Middle East
- Modern facilities
- Military restrictions
- Heat operations
- Night flying popular
